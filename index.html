My Practice & Experience

Clinical Experience

Since establishing my independent practice in Delhi in 2013, I have been dedicated to providing professional, compassionate care. My foundation was built in India and refined in London, UK, where I worked across diverse settings within the National Health Service (NHS) and the voluntary sector, helping individuals navigate complex challenges with clarity and warmth.

Teaching & Workshops

Beyond clinical practice, I am committed to the growth of our field. I have lectured at the post-graduate level, conducted workshops for universities and schools, and I actively assist in professional trainings for Integral Somatic Psychology (ISP) and DARe in India.

Therapeutic Style

My approach is deeply Humanistic—I believe that a safe, respectful, and non-judgemental therapeutic relationship is the essential foundation for healing. I integrate this presence with the analytical precision of evidence-based therapies, helping clients identify deep-seated patterns so they can move towards their goals with ease and resilience.

Recognising that every individual is unique, I tailor my interventions using a diverse range of modalities, including Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), Integral Somatic Psychology (ISP), DARe (Dynamic Attachment Repatterning experience), Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing (EMDR), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), among others.

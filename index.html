Therapeutic Style

As a Counselling Psychologist my work is guided by the Humanistic principles of respect and positive regard and I value the therapeutic relationship.

I predominately work with Cognitive Behavior Therapy and am competent with Cognitive Analytic Therapy.

I occasionally use interventions from other therapies, such as Acceptance and Commitment Therapy, Solution Focused Therapy, EMDR, Mindfulness, Hypnosis, etc. if they fit the client’s case conceptualization and help achieve therapeutic goals.
